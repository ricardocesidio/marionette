How to

  1. Load a PNG — or hit Generate sample character.
  2. Rig: drag on the image to draw a bone. Drag from a bone's tip to chain a child bone. Click selects, Delete removes.
  3. Or skip the manual work: ✨ Auto-detect skeleton analyses the image and places the bones on the character by itself (best with a transparent or plain background). 🧍 Humanoid preset gives the same named bones fitted to the image box instead. Either way, Alt-drag any joint to fine-tune — that works on hand-drawn bones too.
  4. Mirror Rig: rig one side, then ⇄ Mirror bones builds the other across the symmetry axis (drag the axis knob to reposition, or ⌖ to recenter). In Animate, ⇄ Mirror pose / animation flips a pose or a whole walk cycle to the opposite side.
  5. Animate: drag bones to rotate them. Shift-drag the root bone to move the whole character. Keys are saved at the playhead automatically.
  6. Scrub the timeline, press Space to play.
  7. Export the character, or hit ▶ Test in Game.
